YNAB vs Mint vs Monarch: Which One is Best?

Choosing a budgeting app in 2025? Here's the breakdown: YNAB is great for proactive budgeting and learning the 'zero-based' method. Mint is free but being phased out in many markets. Monarch offers clean visuals and excellent goal tracking. If you're a visual planner, Monarch might win. For total control? YNAB all the way.
